Frequently asked questions

Do you do car finance?

We sell for cash at our branches. Buyers may arrange finance with their bank; we assist with paperwork and settlement when collecting the vehicle.

Do you do rent to own?

No — we do not offer rent-to-own plans.

Do you accept trade-ins?

Yes. Bring your vehicle for a free appraisal or start with our online form. Values depend on condition and market demand.

Need help fast?

Call our valuations team at (053) 123-4567 or contact us online to book an appraisal or test drive.
